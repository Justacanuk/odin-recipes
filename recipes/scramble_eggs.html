Scramble Eggs

The perfect Scrambled eggs, butter eggs and stir!

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 2 Tbsp Butter
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste.

Directions

  1. mix eggs thoroughly in a bowl
  2. heat butter in pan until hot.
  3. Add eggs and stir slowly but dont let eggs cook to long in one spot, remove from heat if its cooking to quick
  4. remove and served once eggs are fully cooked
